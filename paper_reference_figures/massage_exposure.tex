\caption{\textbf{Same tasks, opposite verdicts for Massage Therapists.} The
Hatzius--Eloundou (H\&L) measure flags six tasks as exposed to language models
(highlighted). Counting tasks under O*NET core/supplemental weighting, these are
$44\%$ of the occupation. But each fills only a sliver of the workday: weighting
the identical exposure labels by our estimated time-per-task, the exposed share
falls to $16\%$, while the dominant in-person manual tasks remain unexposed ---
moving Massage Therapists out of the highly-exposed category.}
